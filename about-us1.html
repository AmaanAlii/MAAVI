About Us

Manikya International FZ LLC was incorporated in RAK in 2020.

It is a leading company specializing in the trading of precious metals and gem & jewellery. With our extensive experience and expertise in industry, we aim to offer most quality products that are sourced from industry leaders.

VISION

To be referred as the most promising company in the precious metals and Gem & Jewellery Industry.

MISSION

To provide the best quality products to the customers at the right time, at the most competitive price.

The Promoter has in-depth knowledge of this business within experience of 30 years in International Trade. The Company primarily deals in precious metals such as gold, silver etc. as well as Gem and Jewellery.

Customer satisfaction is our top priority, and we strive to build long-term relationships based on trust and reliability.

As a forward-thinking company, we keep up-to-date of the latest technological advancements and prioritize security and confidentiality, ensuring that our clients’ information and transactions are protected using robust measures.

We maintain relations and has business dealings with all major banks and financial institutions as all the major LBMA & non-LBMA gold/silver refineries.
